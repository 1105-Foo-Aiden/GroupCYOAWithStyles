Bad Ending

The raucous night of celebration had gradually unraveled into a chaotic disarray. Laughter and music gave way to hushed conversations and whispered secrets. Everyone seemed to be "leaving," one by one, slipping away from the gathering like shadows retreating from the light. The dim glow of the campfire now cast eerie, elongated shadows across the forest floor, and the muffled sounds of the wilderness seemed to encroach on the isolated clearing.

The alcohol had blurred your senses, leaving you swaying and vulnerable, an easy prey in this remote and foreboding forest. The fear that had been building in the pit of your stomach now loomed over you, suffocating your thoughts. You struggled to maintain your balance, unable to keep up with the sudden stillness of the night.

Suddenly, a hand clamped over your mouth, stifling your scream. Terror surged through your veins as you fought to break free, but it was futile.
