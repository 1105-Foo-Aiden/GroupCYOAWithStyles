Welcome to the CYOA

This is a choose your own adventure agme where your actions dictate the outcome of the story. Each page will have a description of the scene and 2 links to each of your two choices. At the end of your adventure, you will be prompted to play again where you can play again if you wish to get a different ending.


Here is the setting: You and a group of friends went to a cabin in the middle of the woods to escape for the weekend. Your friends are currently outside enjoying the warmth of the campfire they set up, unaware of the dangers lurking in the darkness.
