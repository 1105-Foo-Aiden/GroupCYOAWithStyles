Welcome to the CYOA

This is a choose your own adventure game where your actions dictate the outcome of the story. Each page will have a description of the scene and 2 links that represent your choices in the game. At the end of your adventure, you will be prompted to play again where you can play again if you wish to get a different ending.


Here is the setting: You and a group of friends went to a cabin in the middle of the woods to escape for the weekend- on Halloween, no less! According to a local legend, there is a killer who revisits the area every year on Halloween night to claim more victims. Your friends are currently outside enjoying the warmth of the campfire they set up, unaware of the dangers lurking in the darkness.
